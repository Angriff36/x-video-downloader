  <project_name>X Video Downloader</project_name>

  <overview>
    A cross-platform video downloader application that enables users to download embedded videos from multiple social media and video platforms including X (Twitter), Rumble, Twitch, Reddit, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Vimeo, and Dailymotion. The application consists of a Flutter mobile frontend and a FastAPI backend service deployed on Fly.io, utilizing yt-dlp and ffmpeg for video extraction and processing. The app provides a simple interface for users to paste video URLs, download content directly to their device storage, and access downloaded videos through the device gallery. Key goals include providing reliable video downloading capabilities, maintaining privacy through local-first architecture, ensuring Play Store compliance, and supporting platform-specific optimizations for different video sources.
  </overview>
